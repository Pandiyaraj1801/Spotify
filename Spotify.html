How do I create a playlist?

Playlist are a great way to save collections of music, either for your own listening or to share.


To create one:

1.Tap your Library.
2.Tap CREATE.
3.Give your playlist a name.
4.Start adding songs (and we'll help you along).
How do I activate Data Saver mode?

1.Tap Home.
2.Tap Settings.
3.Tap Datasaver.
4.Switch on Data Saver.

Is is possible to shuffle play music?

Any playlist with the shuffle icon will play on shuffle.

Some playlist won't have the shuffle icon, so you can tap any song to play it.

where can I find podcasts?

Tap Search. Under Browse All, tap Podcasts
